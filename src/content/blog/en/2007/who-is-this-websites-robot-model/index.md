<img src="/legacy-images/images/conscious_robot_crawl.jpg" alt="conscious\_robot\_crawl" />The robot model that you can see in this website header and section summaries is an animatronic human sculpture in bronze and stainless steel designed and built by artist Mark Ho. Its name is ZOHO ARTFORM NO.1.

The copyright owner of the Images of this sculpture is also by Mark Ho. If you are interested in this sculpture and how to get it, please check the following links:

- [ZOHO](http://www.zoho.nl/)
