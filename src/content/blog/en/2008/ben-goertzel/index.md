##### Ben Goertzel

Founder, CEO and Chief Scientist Novamente LLC and Biomind LLC.

Ben research interests include amongst others artificial general intelligence architectures and cognitive science.

_**About Novamente**_

Founded in 2001, Novamente LLC is a 20+ person software company headquartered in Silicon Valley, with a development office near Washington, DC. Novamente’s current and past clients include Northrop-Grumman, the NIH Clinical Center, the CDC, Global Health Exchange, CACI, Object Sciences Corporation, Zero Degrees, Think Passenger and Electric Sheep Company. Novamente virtual agents are powered by Artificial General Intelligence technology based on the company's sophisticated, proprietary Novamente Cognition Engine.
