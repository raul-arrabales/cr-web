##### The 14th annual meeting of the Association for the Scientific Study of Consciousness will be held in Toronto, Canada from Thursday 24th – Sunday 27th, June 2010.

Symposium and Tutorial proposals due Oct. 30th 2009.

Confirmed Speakers:

• Robert Knight (University of California Berkley) • Mohan Matthen (University of British Columbia) • Nicola Clayton (University of Cambridge) • Morris Moscovitch (University of Toronto) • Thomas Metzinger (Johannes Gutenberg-Universität Mainz)

Scientific Program Committee:

• Mel Goodale  (chair) • Mitsuo Kawato • Sean Kelly • Su-Ling Yeh • Petra Stoerig • Evan Thompson • Victor Lamme • Olivia Carter
