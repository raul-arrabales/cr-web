**Vol. 2 No. 1 (June 2010) pp. 75-116** **MACHINE CONSCIOUSNESS: RESPONSE TO COMMENTARIES** _AARON SLOMAN_ DOI: [10.1142/S1793843010000412](http://dx.doi.org/10.1142/S1793843010000412)

**Vol. 2 No. 1 (June 2010) pp. 117-169** **PHENOMENAL AND ACCESS CONSCIOUSNESS AND THE "HARD" PROBLEM: A VIEW FROM THE DESIGNER STANCE** _AARON SLOMAN_ DOI: [10.1142/S1793843010000424](http://dx.doi.org/10.1142/S1793843010000424)
