### Hardwired for Love (Spanish, 2025)

What happens when emotions and machines collide?

In _Amor Programado (Hardwired for Love)_, AI expert Dr. Raúl Arrabales explores how neuroscience, psychology, and artificial intelligence are reshaping the way we understand human nature. From daily interactions with virtual assistants to the possibility of forming deep emotional bonds with machines, this book dives into the big questions of our time:

- Can machines learn to love?

- Could we fall in love with an AI?

- What happens when machines are able to simulate human feelings?

This is more than just a book about technology — it’s an invitation to reflect on the future of love and connection in a world where feelings can be coded. A compelling read for anyone curious about what it means to be human in the age of intelligent machines.
